R U N E r u m i n a t i o n s

The magical stones that reveal the mysteries of your life...

RUNES are alphabetical signs at least eighteen centuries old elaborated by ancient European people. These signs used to be employed in poetry, stone carvings and fortune-telling by many ancient tribes, such as, the Germans, the Celts, and the Vikings.

Although they are an alphabet, the RUNES never became a spoken idiom. Each of its signs is a magical symbol. Therefore, the RUNES were assumed to be a ritual language, becoming the voice of an oracle.

Oracles, however, do not tell the future. Their purpose is to direct people to what is happening inside their minds in order to reach the answers from the subconcience.
